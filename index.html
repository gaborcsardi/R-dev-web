R Developer Page

This site is intended as an intermediate repository for more or less finalized ideas and plans for the R statistical system. Most parts of the site are open to the public, and we welcome discussions on the ideas, but please do not take them for more than that, in particular there is no commitment to actually carry out the plans in finite time unless expressedly stated.

For general information about R see CRAN and its mirror sites.

The core team can access the source files for this site by 


CVSROOT=cvs.r-project.org:/home/rdevel/CVS-ARCHIVE cvs co R-dev-web
any commit to this area is immediately reflected in the web pages.

Release plans

The latest release of R was 1.0.1 on April 14, 2000. We expect this to be the last of the 1.0.x series unless major problems are discovered. The longer term plan is to release 1.1.0 some time in June and have a 1.1.1 in August, ready for the new semester.
