R Developer Page

This site is intended as an intermediate repository for more or less finalized ideas and plans for the R statistical system. Most parts of the site is open to the public, and we welcome discussions on the ideas, but please do not take them for more than that, in particular no commitment to actually carry out the plans in finite time is implied unless expressedly stated. For general information about R see CRAN and its mirror sites. Things to put into the site could be
  • todo lists
  • RFCs
  • various other writeups that might not be quite fit for general publication
  • links to other websites (or copies of those that tend to disappear when you most need them, like Bill Metzenthen's floatingpoint pages...)
  • current release plans and instructions
  • CVS tips
  • CVS logs (if I can figure out how to generate them...)
