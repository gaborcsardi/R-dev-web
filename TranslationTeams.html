R Translation Teams

R version 2.1.0 and later will support translations of program messages into different languages. Instructions for translators are here. A number of translation projects are already underway or completed. To offer your help with one of these, contact a team on the list below. To list your own translation project here, contact Duncan Murdoch (murdoch@stats.uwo.ca) or another member of R-core with the details.
